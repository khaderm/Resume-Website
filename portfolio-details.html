Driving Innovation in Automotive with Mobility: My Role in Toyota Canada's KINTO Initiative.

In the KINTO initiative, our team played a crucial role in developing innovative business models focused on mobility. We shifted the traditional perspective of automotive services to prioritize user experience, emphasizing 'usership' over ownership. This transformation introduced a new paradigm in business strategy. My specific contributions included the development and implementation of a ride-sharing service tailored for our dealership customers, significantly enhancing their operational efficiency. Additionally, I was instrumental in expanding this concept into a comprehensive 'Mobility as a Service' (MaaS) platform, catering to fleet operations on a national scale. This project not only diversified Toyota's service offerings but also established a new standard in automotive mobility solutions.
